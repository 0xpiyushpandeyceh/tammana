About Us

EGV, Academy for English Language is unarguably India’s best-known leading institution where one can learn highest standards of modern day communication from the best instructors. Our exclusivity lies in the fact that EGV is not just meant for students who want to learn basics of the language or excel in academics – be it in school or college, but also for employees who work in private or government organisations and want fluency and complete command over English language.

About the Founder

I am an English language expert with 15 years experience in various coaching institutes in India.And EGV, as an institute, towers above all competitors as an establishment set up with an objective to impart valuable training to aspirants from all strata of society.

Our Vision

1. To reach out to each individual in every nook & corner of the country who need skill development.
2. To make every individual able and capable, by up-scalling his/her skills.
3. To create leaders.
